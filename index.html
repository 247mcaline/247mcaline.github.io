Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide when signing up or purchasing a plan — including your name, email address, payment details, and device information. We also gather data automatically during your use of the service, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and product usage patterns. In some cases, we may receive supplemental data from identity verification partners to power our monitoring features.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to deliver and improve our security services, process payments, send billing and account notifications, detect and respond to threats on your behalf, and send relevant product updates or promotional offers (which you can opt out of at any time). We do not sell your personal information to advertising networks.

3. Data Sharing

We share your data only with carefully vetted service providers who help us run our platform, process transactions, and support customers — all under binding confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information as required by applicable law, to protect our legal rights, or in the event of a business transfer such as a merger or acquisition.

4. Retention & Security

We keep your data for as long as your subscription is active or as required to fulfill legal obligations and resolve any disputes. All data is protected using TLS 1.3 transport encryption and AES-256 storage encryption, in line with current industry best practices.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or limit how we process your personal data. Submit requests to privacy@vaultguard.com and we will respond within 30 days. Residents of California have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what data we hold and to opt out of any data sales.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, preference storage, traffic analytics, and relevance-based messaging. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings or through our in-product cookie preference panel. Some features may be limited if certain cookies are disabled.
